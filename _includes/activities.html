Our Favorite Restaurants

Houston has a diverse selection of wonderful restaurants. Here are some of our suggestions:

  • BBQ: Killen's BBQ (Ranked #2 in the US!), Roegels Barbecue Co (closer to our venue), Rudy's (several locations)

  • Mexican (authentic): Hugo's

  • Texmex: Pappasito's, Jimmy Changas

  • Italian: Vinoteca Poscol

  • Japanese: Izakaya Wa (more authentic), Kata Robata (more modern)

  • Chinese (Sichuan): Chengdu Taste, Cooking Girl

  • Chinese (Dim sum): Crown Seafood

  • Chinese (general): Many in Bellaire Chinatown. Let's be honest, most of you flying in are from places with more amazing Chinese food!

Getting Around

Houston is woefully behind in public transportation, though there is the MetroRail that goes to a few locations. The bus is frankly not used by many people except for commuting to and from work or if they cannot have a car. Renting a car is recommended, especially if you're not staying near the venue. Uber and Lyft are easily accessible throughout the city.
