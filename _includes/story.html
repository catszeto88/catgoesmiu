Our Story

Kevin and Catherine at the Present Company Bar in their hometown of Houston, TX

In the summer of 2012, Kevin and Catherine both logged onto OkCupid.com and matched with each other. Kevin reached out to say hello, and Catherine, emboldened by her incredible trip with friends to San Diego Comic Con, kept messaging back and forth with this cute guy (with a dimple as a bonus!), in hopes of scoring a date. She eagerly told Kevin about her adventures at SDCC, where she had cosplayed as Wonder Woman and the Hulk. Eventually, Kevin finally asked the question Cat was hoping for:

"Would this Hulk like to go out to dinner with me?"
